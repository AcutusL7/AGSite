About QUeen Milling

QUeen Milling is strategically located in Chatham, Ontario, to serve the Canadian and US markets. Our company specializes in manufacturing rice floor and pulse-based alternatives. Our gluten-free products have an industry reputation for superior quality.

Our mill processes raw ingredients without the support of chemicals, chemical clones, added filters or flow agents.

QUeen Milling utilizes lean manufacturing tools and processes to maximize efficiencies. It is central to our company culture to use standardized processes and continuous improvement to provide a winning team mindset at QUeen Milling.

We have been accredited by: Kent Agri and Kosher Certified by the Kashruth Council of Canada

QUeen Milling has a robust Quality Operating System (QOS). Many industry initiatives have been incorporated into the QUeen Milling QOS. They include: Hazard Analysis & Critical Control Points (HACCP), Good Manufacturing Practices (GMPs), Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), cleaning and sanitation, preventative maintenance, traceability, process verification, and training. Our QOS Elements and the Statement of Guarantee provide assurance to our customers that we stand behind our products.

QUeen Milling is a proven industry leader and a supplier of choice. We aim to provide value for our customers in everything we do.
