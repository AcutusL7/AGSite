Welcome to QUeen Milling

QUeen Milling specializes in manufacturing gluten-free flours. Our services include milling, custom blending, co-packaging and private labeling.




Our Vision:

To be a world-class producer of gluten-free flours.




Our Mission:

We are committed to provide superior products and services through a process-driven work environment. We continually strive to exceed customer expectations.




QUeen Milling manufactures various types of rice flour and gluten-free alternatives. Organic gluten-free flours are available as well.

Taste:

QUeen Milling only uses premium ingredients.

Quality, repeatability, and flavour are the key attributes in all our finished products. Our commitment to quality provides our customers with superior products. QUeen Milling understands that it takes a combination of sensations and attentions to make the best in taste. We are driven to provide this in every step of our processes, in every taste and texture combination we produce.

Science:

Our laboratory integrates science into our processes to ensure that we produce top quality products. We manufacture goods to customer specifications in a process-driven work environment. The QUeen Milling Statement of Guarantee provides our customers with a quality assurance guarantee that all goods produced meet or exceed industry standards.

Our Quality team provides extensive physical, organoleptic, and analytical testing to ensure your satisfaction.

Experience:

QUeen Milling is an international food manufacturing company with extensive knowledge and experience in the food industry. We specialize in gluten-free flours and the development of new gluten-free alternatives. As a company, we are committed to expansion in global markets through investments in our people and technology.
